Privacy Policy

Rheum Companion — Managing Life with Chronic Pain

Effective Date: June 12, 2026  |  Last Updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Rheum Companion mobile application ("App", "we", "our", or "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, health data, or user-generated content on our own servers. All workbook entries, symptom logs, and health tool data are stored locally on your device and are never sent to our servers.

The only data processing that occurs is through our third-party service providers:

  • Subscription & Payment Data: If you purchase a subscription, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We use RevenueCat Inc. to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt to verify your subscription. We do not have access to your credit card number, billing address, or other financial details.
  • AI Chat Messages: When you use the AI Therapist Guide feature, your chat messages are routed through Cloudflare Inc. (Worker proxy) to Google LLC (Gemini API) for processing. These messages are processed temporarily to generate responses.
  • Basic Device Information: RevenueCat may collect basic device identifiers (such as a vendor ID) solely for subscription management purposes. This data is not used for advertising or tracking.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or account credentials
  • Health or medical information (stored locally only)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers

3. How Your Data Is Used

The limited data processed through our third-party providers is used exclusively to:

  • Verify and manage your subscription status
  • Process payments through Apple or Google
  • Provide AI-powered support through Google's Gemini API
  • Provide customer support for subscription-related issues

4. Third-Party Services & Data Retention

Our App uses the following third-party services, each with their own data retention and deletion policies:

RevenueCat Inc. (Subscription Management)

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: Payment processing for iOS. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Store: Payment processing for Android. Google Privacy Policy

Cloudflare Inc. (Worker Proxy)

  • Data Processed: AI chat messages (routed temporarily)
  • Data Retention: Messages are not stored; processed only in transit
  • Deletion Requests: No data stored, so no deletion necessary
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy

Google LLC (Gemini API)

  • Data Processed: AI chat messages
  • Data Retention: Messages may be retained for up to 180 days for safety and improvement purposes
  • Deletion Requests: Follow Google's instructions at Google Account Data & Privacy to request deletion
  • Google Privacy Policy

Apple App Store (iOS Payments)

  • Apple Privacy Policy

Google Play Store (Android Payments)

  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage & Security

All app content (workbook entries, symptom logs, medication records, health tool data, symptom scores, and joint maps) is stored locally on your device only. If you uninstall the App, this data will be permanently deleted. We do not maintain backups of your local data. Symptom scores and joint maps leave your device only when you choose to export them.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete. To manage your subscription, use the subscription management features in the Apple App Store or Google Play Store settings on your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
