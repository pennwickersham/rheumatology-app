Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Your subscription is managed through your device's app store. To manage or cancel:

iOS: Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Rheum Companion.

Android: Open Google Play Store → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → select Rheum Companion.

Is my data stored on your servers?

No. All of your data — including workbook entries, symptom logs, and health records — is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your personal health information. If you uninstall the app, your locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open the app on your new device and tap "Restore Purchase" on the subscription screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account that was used for the original purchase.

I'm having trouble with the subscription purchase. What should I do?

Please try the following steps:

1. Ensure you have a stable internet connection.

2. Make sure your Apple ID or Google account has a valid payment method.

3. Close and reopen the app, then try again.

4. If the issue persists, contact us at info@brewsterwickershampublications.com and we'll help resolve it.

What does the free trial include?

New subscribers receive a 7-day free trial with full access to all premium features, including the 25-module digital workbook, AI therapist guide, and health tools suite. You won't be charged during the trial period. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be billed.

What devices are supported?

Rheum Companion is available on iPhone and iPad running iOS 15 or later, and Android devices running Android 6.0 or later.

Is this app a substitute for medical treatment?

No. Rheum Companion is a self-help wellness tool designed to complement — not replace — professional medical care. The content is educational and informational in nature. Always consult with your healthcare provider regarding your health and treatment options.
